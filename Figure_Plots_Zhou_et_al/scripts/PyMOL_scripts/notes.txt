Here are some notes on the files in each directory.

Figure 6A
9bde_LDLR_APOB_only.pse is the output from Arpeggio. 
9bde is the PDB id for the LDLR-APOB costructure, subset to the beta-belt of APOB and the class A repeats of LDLR. 

fig_6A_script.pml contains all of the information to configure the aesthetics of the predicted interatom contacts and position the camera. 

LDLR_APOB.png is a copy of the final image used in Figure 6A.

9bde_LDLR_APOB_only_final.pse is 9bde_LDLR_APOB_only.pse with the .pml script run on it. 

Figure 6B

APOB_LDLR_S213_wt.pse, etc. are all outputs from Arpeggio.

The .pml scripts are for adjusting the aesthetics of interatom contacts and positioning the camera. 

All files with _final are the corresponding .pse file with the .pml file run on it. 

The images are the final ones used in the generation of figure 6B.
